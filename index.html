Howl's Moving Castle


Howl’s Moving Castle is a fantasy story about Sophie, a young woman turned into an old lady by a curse, who finds adventure and love in the magical walking castle of the wizard Howl.

The Wind Rises


The Wind Rises is a historical drama about Jirō Horikoshi, a young man who dreams of designing airplanes, as he pursues his passion for aviation while facing love, loss, and the harsh realities of war.

Spirited Away


Spirited Away is about Chihiro, a young girl who gets trapped in a mysterious spirit world and must work at a magical bathhouse to save her parents and find her way back home.

My Neighbor Totoro


My Neighbor Totoro is a heartwarming story about two sisters who move to the countryside and befriend Totoro, a gentle forest spirit who helps them through magical adventures and family challenges.
